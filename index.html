About

Tela Labs is an exclusive Computer Science & Engineering club, which is fueled by people like you! Based on your survey response, we want to develop multiple programming and designing communities in order for you to enhance your industrial and networking skills at an undergraduate level. We function on a transparent yet interactive platform where you can share your awesome ideas, projects, or experiences. You can work with your friends or professors on projects to explore your interests in Computer Science. If you don't have any prior experience in coding or design but you are interested, you are always welcome to join Tela Labs and learn with us!
